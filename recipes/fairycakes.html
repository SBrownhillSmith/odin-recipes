Fairy Cakes

Description

Everyone's favourite easy recipe. Fun to try with the kids, decorate any way you like or add different flavours into the mix.

Ingredients

  • 4oz butter or margarine, softened at room temperature
  • 4oz caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4oz self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 160degrees. Line a 12 hole cake tray with cake cases
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together in a bowl until pale. Beat in the eggs, a little at a time, and stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. Fold in the flour using a large metal spoon. Add a little milk until the mixture is a soft dropping consistency and spoon the mixture into the paper cases until they are half full.
